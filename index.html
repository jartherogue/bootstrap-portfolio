Jason Reeves

About Me

Jason Reeves was born in San Jose, CA in 1983 to Clayton and Debbie Reeves (nee Parks.) Clay Reeves was a hardware engineer with IBM, and Debbie was an administrative assistant for Allied Sysco.

Jason also has a little brother, Jeffrey Reeves, who was born in 1985 and is currently studying to become a R.N. He graduated from Brighton High School in 2001 and then the University of Utah in 2005. He then moved to Colorado and worked for Enterprise Rent-A-Car until 2010, when he took a position with eBay Motors.

Jason spends weekends golfing, playing video games, and enjoying time with his children.
